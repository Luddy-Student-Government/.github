## About LSG

The **Luddy Student Government** represents the student body of the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering. Our mission is to advocate for students, collaborate with faculty and administration, and foster a strong, connected, and supportive Luddy community.

LSG works to:
- Serve as the voice of Luddy students  
- Collaborate with faculty and administration on policies and initiatives  
- Strengthen relationships between students and faculty  
- Organize social, academic, and professional events  
- Support and collaborate with Luddy student organizations  
- Distribute funding to approved Luddy-sponsored organizations  
- Promote Luddy within the university and beyond  
- Improve the overall student experience at Luddy
